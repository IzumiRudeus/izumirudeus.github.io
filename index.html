FURINA

Real name Furina de Fontaine is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact who can alternate between Pneuma and Ousia alignments.

Furina is introduced as the bombastic and sometimes overconfident Hydro Archon. However, this is revealed to be a public persona necessitated by the prophecy, and after the truth is exposed, she discards her guise and dedicates to live a much humbler life.
